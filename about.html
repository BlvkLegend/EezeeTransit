Who We Are

A Company Built on One Idea

Eezee Transit was founded in Lagos with a straightforward idea: the logistics and transport market in Nigeria had plenty of options but a real shortage of consistency. Pickups happened late. Prices changed at the last moment. Customers were left guessing.

We set out to fix that by putting three things at the centre of every job: vetted drivers, GPS-equipped vehicles, and direct communication at every step. Not as premium add-ons. As the baseline.

Reliability is not a premium feature. It is the least a business should expect from any transport partner.

Today we handle courier deliveries, freight transport, car and bus rentals, and corporate logistics across Lagos and major Nigerian routes. Every service is backed by the same standard regardless of job size.

We are a small team with big standards. We would rather do fewer jobs perfectly than many jobs carelessly. That philosophy is why clients come back.
