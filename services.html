Freight Transport

Move Heavy Loads Without the Headache

When your cargo is too large for a courier and too important to leave to chance, our freight service is the answer. We handle palletized goods, large pallet loads, and long-haul cargo with trucks, lift-gate equipment, and experienced drivers across Nigeria.

Real-Time GPS Monitoring

Every freight vehicle is GPS-tracked. Our team monitors the route live and contacts you ahead of any delay before it becomes a problem.

Interstate and Long-Haul Routes

We run regular routes between Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Ibadan. Volume discounts apply for businesses with consistent shipment frequency.

Lift-Gate Trucks Available

Loading heavy pallets without a forklift on-site is not a problem. Ask for a lift-gate truck when you request your quote.

Vehicle Rentals

The Right Vehicle, with a Driver, When You Need It

Renting a vehicle with us means more than just a car. You get a vetted professional who shows up on time, drives safely, and treats your passengers with respect. Hourly or daily rates, no self-drive complications.

Choose from sedans and SUVs for business errands, minivans and minibuses for small groups, or full coaches for large events. Every rental includes a licensed driver so you can focus on the journey.

Professional Licensed Drivers

Hourly and Daily Rates
